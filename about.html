About Us

Creating Benchmarks, Crossing Milestones

The growth of the automotive and engineering industry around Pune has led to an increased demand for precision heavy machining and fabrication services.
Meeting moments of challenge with innovative ideas, global technology and great teamwork, today we are a frontline player in the Heavy Precision Machining and Fabrication industry.

Our business stands on a solid foundation of integrity and technical competency. Established in 2006, we have earned the trust of a growing clientele with our commitment to quality workmanship and achieved healthy year-on-year growth. Since 2012, we are operating out a spacious production facility at Markal, Pune, and our corporate office is located in Nigdi, Pune.
At Moraya Industries, we fulfill this escalating need through our operations rooted in reliability.

CORE VALUES

  • Passion for precision machining is our driving force
  • Integrity is the cornerstone of our business
  • Excellence of our people is paramount to us
  • Eco-friendly practices are embedded in our operations
